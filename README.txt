Thank you for choosing the ClientEngage Project Platform.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ClientEngage is a fully-featured project management platform which helps you 
keep your clients in the loop when working with them.
The benefits of ClientEngage are greater interaction with and engagement of your 
clients during your development lifecycles.
